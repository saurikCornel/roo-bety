Rules of the Game "Roo Bety"

Welcome to "Roo Bety," an exciting game where you control a playful kangaroo jumping across floating objects in a river! Each successful leap brings you closer to mini-games filled with fun challenges. Are you ready for this wild adventure?

How to Play

The game is simple yet challenging. Here's how it works:

  • Jumping: Tap the screen to make the kangaroo jump onto floating objects. Time your jumps carefully to avoid falling into the water!
  • Floating Objects: These platforms vary in size and stability. Some may move or disappear after landing on them.
  • Lives: You start with three lives. If you fall into the water, you lose one life. The game ends when all lives are lost.

Mini-Games

Once you successfully cross the river, you unlock thrilling mini-games set in different environments:

  • Circus – Tic-Tac-Toe: Test your strategy skills against the computer or a friend in this classic game.
  • Desert – Find the Pair: Sharpen your memory by finding matching pairs hidden under tiles in a vibrant desert setting.
  • Mountains – Sliding Puzzle: Solve a challenging sliding puzzle featuring breathtaking mountain landscapes.

Advanced Tips

  • Pay attention to the movement patterns of floating objects to time your jumps perfectly.
  • Stay calm under pressure—rushing can lead to mistakes!
  • Practice regularly to improve your timing and reflexes.

Game Modes

  • Adventure Mode: Journey through various levels, each with unique obstacles and challenges.
  • Time Trial Mode: Race against the clock to see how far you can get in a limited amount of time.
  • Endless Mode: Push your limits in this infinite challenge where difficulty increases as you progress.

Embark on a colorful journey with "Roo Bety." From leaping across rivers to mastering mini-games, every moment is packed with excitement and joy. Can you conquer all challenges and become the ultimate Roo Master?
